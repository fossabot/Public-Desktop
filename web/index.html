About

Public Desktop is an application for managing content with speed and power. Acting as a replacement for window's explorer, running your applications and sharing your content has never been easier. Call friends, share and run content, and manage your system by harnessing the true power of your PC.

Using Peer to Peer communication, create and host spaces, like websites, with whomever you choose. Create fast content with speed using the built in editor, or share powerful dynamic content using the power of Adobe Flash and Actionscript 3.

Reclaim control of your PC. This lightweight application will be your PC's new explorer that doesn't take up massive amounts of processing power. Launch programs, compute process intensive tasks, and get your work or play done with new, greater speeds.
